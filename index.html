About Us

At the IUST AI Lab, we explore the intersection of artificial intelligence, neuroscience, and signal processing. Our mission is to develop intelligent systems that can understand, learn, and interact with the world through advanced algorithms and computational models inspired by the human brain.

Research Areas

  • Brain–Computer Interfaces (BCI) and ECoG decoding
  • Deep learning for biomedical signal processing
  • Generative and state-space models for sequential data
  • Computational neuroscience and cognitive modeling
  • AI safety and interpretability
